Course 1

Basic course

This course will help your mind to release all the tensions and stress and let you concentrate and focus on things better. Concentration exercises help sharpen the mind and improve the ability to concentrate. It is said that 10 minutes of concentration does a lot of good to the mind an any irritation or bad mood due to lack of concentration or focus in work can be eliminated.

  • Duration: 10 mins

Course 2

Breathing Exercises

Taking just a few moments each day to practice some deep breathing exercises can decrease stress, relax your mind, body, help you sleep better and also lighten your mood. Breathing correctly is important for your overall well being. It improves blood flow and increases energy level.

  • Duration: 10 minutes

COURSE 3

Yoga exercises

Yoga is a physical exercise. Many people use yoga therapy to manage mental and emotional problems. Yoga has the ability to improve mood and lessen anxiety compared to walking and other forms of exercise. This is due to the release of a brain chemical.

  • Duration: 10 minutes

COURSE 4

Positive Thinking

Positive minds are powerful minds. We can do and achieve almost anything with a mind that has the willingness and determination to do it. Optimism brings advantages to many aspects of life like health, relationships, career and personal goals.

  • Duration: 10 minutes

course 5

Meditation

Meditation is relaxation. Regular practice of meditation has profound benefits on the core three areas of our existence-physical, mental and spiritual. Meditation decreases anxiety, improves emotional stability, increases happiness, relaxes the mind and helps to gain clarity and peace of mind.

  • Duration: 10 minutes

course 6

Advance Course

The advance course is an amalgamation of all the exercises which starts from relaxing your body, then mind and then soul giving you complete relaxation. It has a lot of powerful meditation which will help you delve deeper into yourself.

  • Duration: 20 minutes
